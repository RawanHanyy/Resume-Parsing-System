Job Title: iOS Developer

Location: Cairo, Egypt
Job Type: Full-time

Job Summary
We are looking for a skilled iOS Developer to design, build, and maintain high-quality iPhone and iPad applications. The ideal candidate should have strong experience with Swift, iOS frameworks, mobile architecture patterns, API integration, and App Store release processes.

Key Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain iOS applications using Swift and modern iOS frameworks.
- Build clean, reusable, and scalable mobile features.
- Collaborate with backend developers, UI/UX designers, QA engineers, and product managers.
- Integrate RESTful APIs and third-party SDKs.
- Optimize application performance, responsiveness, and stability.
- Debug and resolve crashes, memory issues, and UI defects.
- Write unit tests and participate in code reviews.
- Prepare builds for testing, release, and App Store deployment.
- Follow best practices in architecture, code quality, and version control.

Required Skills
- Strong proficiency in Swift
- Good experience with UIKit and/or SwiftUI
- Knowledge of MVVM, MVC, or Clean Architecture
- Experience integrating REST APIs and handling JSON
- Familiarity with Core Data, Firebase, or Realm
- Experience with Git and collaborative development workflows
- Understanding of app lifecycle, asynchronous programming, and memory management
- Experience with Xcode, TestFlight, and App Store Connect

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with Combine
- Experience with push notifications and in-app purchases
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines
- Knowledge of Objective-C is a plus
- Published one or more apps on the App Store

Experience Required
- 2+ years of professional iOS development experience

Education
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field is preferred
